About Us

Jane Jones

Hidden San Diego is a web site created in late 2010 by Jane Jones a creative person who has special interest in hidden places in San Diego.

She is an adventurer and intrigued by the unknown, constantly seeking out to explore new places.

The goal of the web site is to present a place for people who are interested in hiking to connect with each other, as well as providing detail information about hiking places in San Diego.

Hidden San Diego web site is user friendly and responsive on all devices for the user to explore and enjoy.

The site offers photos and reviews of hiking places, Hidden San Diego takes pride in presenting the updated information as well as sending monthly e-newsletter to its members.

Hidden San Diego website is the result of her combined adventurist experiences with her skills and love for photography and her semi-skills for web creation.
